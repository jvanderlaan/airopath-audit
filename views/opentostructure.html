Open to Structure

This is when WAPs are installed in open warehouse environments, typically the WAP is suspended down to 20’-0” AFF. It could be totally open, ground level pallet, deep library shelving, open AR fields, misc. modules/stations, etc. For all of the above, these situations should be called out.

Airopath Tolerances

· WAP may be installed up to 10’-0” away from its intended location as shown in the design plans.

· WAP may not be the lowest object in elevation as long as the other object is more than 15’-0” away from the WAP.

· Tech does not need to call out WAP mounting material (strut, rod, etc.) for being within a horizontal clearance of lights and sprinkler heads if the WAP itself is suspended down significantly. Please use a reasoned approach to this.
  • Broadcast
    • WAP is not powered (IT)
    • WAP is not broadcasting (IT)
    • WAP is broadcasting incorrect name (IT)
    • WAP is broadcasting MAC address (IT)
  • Equipment
    • WAP is missing (LVC)
    • WAP model is incorrect (IT)
    • [MIST] WAP not aligned with true north (LVC)
  • Label
    • Label is missing (LVC)
    • Label shows incorrect name (LVC)
    • Label is not legible (LVC)
  • Cabling
    • Service loop is below WAP (LVC)
    • Service loop is resting on backboard (Attention)
    • Service loop is zip tied (Attention)
  • Backboard
    • Backboard is missing (LVC)
    • Backboard is severely damaged (LVC)
    • Backboard is not level with the ground (LVC)
  • Safety Cable
    • Safety cables are missing entirely (LVC)
    • Safety cable for WAP-->backplate-->backboard is missing (LVC)
    • Safety cable for mount assembly-->structure is missing (LVC)
    • Safety cable not visible from ground (Attention)
  • Mount Assembly
    • Threaded rod suspended greater than 5’-0” (LVC)
    • Strut between structural beams lacks two points of contact (LVC)
    • [RETRO] Stabilization material is missing (LVC)
  • Elevation
    • WAP install elevation significantly deviates from cut sheet (LVC)
    • WAP is not the lowest object (LVC)
  • Location
    • WAP install location deviates from plan (LVC)
  • Obstruction
    • Backboard is within 12’-0” from Big Ass Fan, measured horizontally (LVC)
    • Backboard is within 5’-0” from object greater than 2’-0” wide (LVC)
